Ideas to Flip-up Instagram

The best way to make your post viral is to make it worthy. Following are a few pointers:

  1. Mine positive things about your products and services. Highlight them in your posts.
  2. Add inspirational quotes to compel people to read your post.
  3. Add appealing images or videos. Visuals speak more than thousand words.
  4. Lay a call-to-action for a definite purpose.
  5. Compel users to generate content for you by engaging them in your post. Repost fan created content on your Instagram account.
